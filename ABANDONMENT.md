# Cyber Fidget — Abandonment Clause

This document describes what happens to the Cyber Fidget project if it is no longer actively maintained.

## Trigger Conditions

The project is considered abandoned if **both** of the following are true:

1. **No commits** to any official Cyber Fidget repository for **24 consecutive months**.
2. **No explicit statement** from Dismo Industries LLC declaring continued maintenance.

Alternatively, Dismo Industries LLC may issue an **explicit abandonment declaration** at any time.

## What Happens Upon Abandonment

If the project is abandoned:

- **All firmware source code** remains available under GPL-3.0 (it already is).
- **PCB design source files** (schematics, layout, BOM) will be released under the **CERN Open Hardware Licence v2 — Strongly Reciprocal (CERN-OHL-S-2.0)**.
- **Documentation** remains under CC BY-SA 4.0.

## What Does NOT Transfer

- The **"Cyber Fidget" trademark** does not transfer. It remains the property of Dismo Industries LLC.
- **Commercial licensing agreements** with third parties are unaffected.

## Acquirer Inheritance

If Dismo Industries LLC transfers the project to a new maintainer or organization, the new owner inherits all obligations in this document, including this abandonment clause.

## Activity Heartbeat

To provide a verifiable signal of active maintenance, Dismo Industries LLC will make at least one commit per year to the [cyberfidget-docs](https://github.com/CyberFidget/cyberfidget-docs) repository confirming the project's active status.
